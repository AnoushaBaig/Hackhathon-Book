# AI Chatbot Assistant

Welcome to our AI-powered chatbot assistant! This intelligent assistant is designed to help you navigate and understand the content of the Physical-AI-Humanoid Robots book.

## How It Works

Our AI assistant uses advanced language models to understand your questions and provide relevant answers based on the book's content. The chatbot can help you:

- Find specific information in the book
- Explain complex concepts in simpler terms
- Provide examples and use cases
- Navigate to relevant sections

## Getting Started

The chatbot is available throughout the website via the floating chat button in the bottom right corner of your screen. Simply click the button to start a conversation.

## Features

- **Context-Aware Responses**: The chatbot understands the context of your questions based on the book's content
- **Real-Time Assistance**: Get instant answers to your questions
- **Knowledge Base**: Access to the entire book's content for comprehensive answers
- **Conversational Interface**: Natural language interaction for easy communication

## Best Practices

- Ask specific questions for better answers
- If you don't get the answer you're looking for, try rephrasing your question
- The chatbot works best with questions related to AI, robotics, and the content covered in this book
